Carlos Paredes

Time Line of
Carlos Paredes' life:

  • 1925 - Was born on February 16, 1925 in Coimbra;
  • 1929 - Started studying Portuguese guitar at age 4 with his father;
  • 1934 - Moves to Lisbon where dedicated completely to the study of guitar;
  • 1949 - Carlos Paredes begins in 1949 a regular collaboration in a program of Artur Paredes in the National Broadcaster and finishes the secondary studies in a private college. He did not finish high school and enrolled in the singing lessons of the Portuguese Musical Youth, becoming, in 1949, administrative officer of the Hospital de São José;
  • 1958 - Is arrested by PIDE for opposing Salazar, is accused of belonging to the Portuguese Communist Party, of which he was in fact militant, being released in late 1959 and expelled from the civil service, following a trial;
  • 1962 - Is invited by the director Paulo Rocha, to compose the soundtrack of the movie "Os Verdes Anos";
  • 1992 - He was made Commander of the Military Order of Sant'Iago da Espada;
  • 2003 - A disease of the central nervous system (myelopathy) prevented him from performing during the last 11 years of his life; 
  • 2004 - Died July 23, 2004.

When I die, my guitar also dies. My father used to say that, when he died, he would like that his guitar would be broken and buried with him. I would like to do the same. If I have to die.

Carlos Paredes
